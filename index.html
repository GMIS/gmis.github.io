Welcome to GMIS

中文版

GMIS（General Machine Intelligent System）is a general robot brain (or operation system). All intelligent hardware platform based on GMIS will become robot, and allows users use quasi nature luanguage to order the robot to execute program that compiled by robot itself. In this way, robots will provide users with personalize services, and it will have autonomous intelligence in the future.Click here to find more technical description.
