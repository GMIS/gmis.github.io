General Machine Intelligent System

Welcome to GMIS

中文版

General Machine Intelligent System(GMIS）is a general robot operating system,which can turn all of your devices into robots. By using quasi- natural language, it gives ordinary users freedom to drive robots working programmatically to meet user's individualized needs. Our technological breakthroughs will be able to achieve the autonomous intelligence of GMIS in the future .Click here to view more technical details.

After 12 years for research and development, we have got a GMIS prototype.

For more intuitive to show the features of GMIS and the prototype, we recorded some demo videos.

  • https://player.vimeo.com/video/112729494 This video only has 2 minutes, in which we try to use GMIS with a machine arm. The arm has completed the crowdfunding in Kickstarter.

  • https://player.vimeo.com/video/112729495 This video has 18 minutes, we showed a practical case of GMIS using external object.

  • https://player.vimeo.com/video/113364683 This video has 45 minutes, which similar to the above video but added some explanations about GMIS' design philosophy.
